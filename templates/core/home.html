{# SECTION 4 — ROOT CAUSES #}
Root Causes

Skin health starts from within

Lumina analyses 6 key root causes behind your skin issues — going deeper than any store-bought test.

💧
Hydration Level

Dehydrated skin looks dull, feels tight and is prone to fine lines. Your moisture barrier needs targeted care.

🔬
Acne & Breakouts

Excess sebum, clogged pores, and bacterial overgrowth cause acne. AI maps severity across 5 facial zones precisely.

🌡️
Skin Undertone

Warm, cool or neutral — your undertone dictates which makeup shades and skincare actives work best for you.

⚡
Pigmentation & Tone

Sun damage, hormones and inflammation trigger uneven pigmentation. Early detection allows targeted treatment.

🧬
Skin Aging

Loss of collagen, elastin and cellular turnover accelerates visible aging. Your skin age may differ from your real age.

🌿
Skin Type

Oily, dry, combination or normal — your skin type determines the ideal cleanser, moisturiser and treatment routine.
